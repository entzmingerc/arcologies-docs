Instructions

  1. From the norns parameters menu, select < SAVE MAP.
  2. Browse to /dust/data/arcologies/maps and locate your .txt file.
  3. Open the map in a text editor and copy and paste it into the input above.
  4. Resize your browser window to position the water color.
  5. Use your operating system's screenshot function to capture.
  6. If you don't follow the format exactly it won't work. No validation, etc. :)
  7. You can also dream up new arcologies by just typing into the input.
